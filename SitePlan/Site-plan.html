Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to advertise B&B Tax Associates. We want to point out why clients should choose B&B Tax above other companies. This will benefit B&B Tax by bringing in more customers and revenue.

Audience

The target audience is working americans who need help with their taxes.
